About Us

Located in the heart of Sheridan, Just a Card Shop is your friendly neighborhood hub for trading card games and collectibles. Whether you're looking to expand your Magic: The Gathering deck, find that rare Pokémon card, or browse through our sports card collection — we've got something for every collector and player.

We also offer a dedicated play space for community gaming, and regularly host tournaments, release events, and casual game nights. Everyone is welcome, from beginners to seasoned players!
